Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Giant pandas (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.
  • Pandas have 6 toes to grasp bamboo.
  • Pandas like to lick copper (Cu) and iron (Fe).

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  3. Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  4. Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!
  5. Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
